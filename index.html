Utah

Utah is a state in the western United States. It is borded by Colorado to the east, Wyoming to the northeast, Idaho to the north, Arizona to the south, and Nevada to the west. It also touches a corner of New Mexico in the southeast. The state is a center of transporation, education, information technology, and research, and a major tourist destination for outdoor receration. Salt Lake City, the state capital and largest city, is home to the headquartes of the church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church).
